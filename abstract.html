ABSTRACT

The main objective of this project is to conduct a survey to identification of people who are under below poverty line (BPL) in an area .The village selected for conducting the survey is MULUMUDI village, NELLORE RURAL, NELLORE DISTRICT, ANDHRAPRADESH. We have critically analyzed all the previous considerations for BPL identifications but still we found the gap to consider few essential factors for BPL identification in rural area. Finally, we have discussed our finding through critical analysis and proposed the few essential factors that are vital to be considering for BPL identification of rural area.
